About POPX

POPX is a custom suite of GPU-based particle, geometry, and simulation operators for TouchDesigner, developed by Yuval Cohen.

Built from the ground up with performance and flexibility in mind, POPX combines high-performance compute shaders with intuitive TouchDesigner-style interfaces to provide powerful tools for procedural geometry, particle systems, simulations, and real-time visual effects.

Developer

Yuval Cohen is a creative technologist and TouchDesigner developer specializing in real-time graphics, procedural systems, and interactive installations.

With a passion for pushing the boundaries of GPU computing in creative contexts, Yuval develops tools that make complex particle systems and simulations accessible to artists and developers.
