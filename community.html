Support POPX on Patreon

Become a Patron

Early Access

Support the ongoing development of POPX operators by becoming a patron. Your contribution helps fund new features, bug fixes, and regular updates.

What You Get:

  • Early access to new operators — Test and use new features before public release
  • Download POPX releases — Access all current and future POPX operator packages
  • Priority support — Get faster responses to bug reports and feature requests
  • Exclusive tutorials and examples — Patron-only content and sample projects
  • Influence development — Vote on which features to prioritize
Join Patreon

Not ready to commit to a subscription? That's okay! All POPX operators will remain free and open source. Patreon supporters simply get early access and help sustain development.

Join the Discord Community

POPX Discord Server

Active Community

Connect with other POPX users, share your projects, ask questions, and get real-time help from the community and developers.

What You'll Find:

  • #general — General discussion about POPX and TouchDesigner
  • #help — Get assistance with operator setup and troubleshooting
  • #showcase — Share your projects and setups using POPX
  • #feature-requests — Suggest new operators or improvements
  • #tutorials — Community-created guides and tips
  • #announcements — Stay updated on new releases and updates
Join Discord

The Discord community is the fastest way to get help, share ideas, and connect with other TouchDesigner artists working with POPX. Whether you're just getting started or building advanced simulations, everyone is welcome!

Community Guidelines

To keep the POPX community welcoming and productive, please follow these guidelines:

  • Be respectful and constructive in all interactions
  • Search existing messages before asking questions that may have been answered
  • Share knowledge and help others when you can
  • Keep discussions relevant to POPX, TouchDesigner, and creative coding
  • Don't spam, self-promote excessively, or share pirated content
