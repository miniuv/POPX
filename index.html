Welcome to POPX Docs

The official documentation for the POPX family of operators — a custom suite of GPU-based particle, geometry, and simulation components for TouchDesigner.

POPX combines high-performance compute shaders with intuitive controls to provide powerful tools for procedural geometry, particle systems, simulations, and more.

Operator Families

Generators

Create new particles, geometry, or fields from scratch.

Modifiers

Transform, move, or modify existing particle and geometry data.

Falloffs

Define influence regions, weight masks, and spatial constraints.

Tools

Utilities for visualization, analysis, conversion, and debugging.

Simulations

Physics-based solvers including FLIP, SPH, and RayPOP systems.
